Info

What is a Hackathon?

A hackathon is a social coding event where participants will create their own projects and submit them to be judged for prizes.

When is it?

Hackstoga will take place on Saturday, April 2nd, 2022 from 9am to 6pm.

Where will the event be?

Conestoga High School. (200 Irish Road, Berwyn PA)

How much does it cost?

The event is completely free! Plus, we will be providing snacks and lunch all throughout the day.

Do I have to bring anything?

All you need is a computer and a charger.

How will we be judged?

Judges and criteria will be release closer to the time of the event.

Can I join with no coding experience?

While some coding experience is recommended, HackStoga is ultimately a place to learn. So even if you don’t have that much programming experience, HackStoga is for you!

Do I need a team?

You can compete individually or in teams of up to 4.

How many teams can join per school?

One team from each school will be allowed as priority sign-ups for HackStoga. Secondary teams will be allowed to sign up, but they will be admitted based on availability. Also, for those wondering, you actually do not need to bring a teacher if you're from another school.
